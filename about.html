About.

I'm Jamie - a final-year Psychology PhD student at Keele University. My work focuses around memory, cognition, and ageing, and I am also particularly interested in brain imaging.


I am not an R expert by any stretch of the imagination, and I only started investing any time into programming within the last few years. But still, I'm hoping these guides will help to put a few people on the right track and get started - it's well worth the time investment!

I love photography and astrophotography (fellow photographers check out my Flickr!), video games, and travel - a personal highlight would be getting to stay overnight in Chernobyl.


If you have any comments, questions, or just feel the need to say hi, you can contact me at: j.adams@keele.ac.uk
